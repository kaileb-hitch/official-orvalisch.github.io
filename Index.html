About the Language

Ørvalisch (ørvalisch) is a constructed linguistic system built upon robust strategic, linguistic, and cultural philosophies[cite: 301, 486, 513]. Visually anchored by its official banner, the language features intricate grammatical distinctions, gender-based temporal parameters [cite: 329, 333, 335], and deep conceptual roots inspired by the game of chess (scatra)[cite: 512, 513].
